# Code of Conduct

## Our standard
Please keep collaboration respectful, constructive, and focused on improving the toolkit.

## Expected behavior
- Be specific and professional.
- Critique ideas clearly, not people.
- Explain architectural concerns with evidence.
- Keep discussions grounded in the repository's purpose.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Personal attacks
- Harassment
- Bad-faith review behavior
- Repeatedly ignoring the repository's foundation-vs-overlay design

## Enforcement
Repository maintainers may remove content or restrict participation if behavior harms collaboration.
